About

I work where scientific evidence, medical images, and intelligent systems meet.

I am a Biomedical Engineering researcher advised by Dr. Ruogu Fang. My work focuses on making medical AI useful at the individual-patient level—from label-efficient segmentation to neuroimaging biomarkers and agentic systems that expose their evidence and reasoning.

Outside the lab, I enjoy hiking, scuba diving, skiing, photography, and exploring new recipes.

“The goal is not only to predict, but to make the next scientific and clinical question clearer.”
